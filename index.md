# Brilbox Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: February 5, 2026**

Brilbox ("we", "us", or "our") operates mobile applications (the "Apps"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Apps.

## Information We Collect

### Device Information
- Device identifiers (Firebase Instance ID, PlayFab device ID)
- Device model and operating system version

### Usage Data
- App interaction data (screen views, feature usage)
- Session information

### Diagnostics
- Crash logs and error reports
- App performance data

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for:
- **App Functionality**: To provide save/backup features via PlayFab
- **Analytics**: To understand how users interact with our Apps and improve user experience
- **Bug Fixes**: To identify and fix crashes and technical issues

## Third-Party Services

Our Apps use the following third-party services:
- **Firebase Analytics**: For usage analytics
- **Firebase Crashlytics**: For crash reporting
- **PlayFab**: For cloud save/backup functionality

These services may collect information used to identify you. Please refer to their respective privacy policies.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties.

## Data Security

All data is transmitted securely using HTTPS encryption.

## Children's Privacy

Our Apps do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

## Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:
