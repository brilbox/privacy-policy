# Privacy Policy
**Last Updated: February 5, 2026**
Brilbox ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Dungeon Merge mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App.
## Information We Collect
### Device Information
- Device identifiers (Firebase Instance ID, PlayFab device ID)
- Device model and operating system version
### Usage Data
- App interaction data (screen views, feature usage)
- Session information
### Diagnostics
- Crash logs and error reports
- App performance data
## How We Use Your Information
We use the collected data for:
- **App Functionality**: To provide save/backup features via PlayFab
- **Analytics**: To understand how users interact with our App and improve user experience
- **Bug Fixes**: To identify and fix crashes and technical issues
## Third-Party Services
Our App uses the following third-party services:
- **Firebase Analytics**: For usage analytics
- **Firebase Crashlytics**: For crash reporting
- **PlayFab**: For cloud save/backup functionality
These services may collect information used to identify you. Please refer to their respective privacy policies.
## Data Sharing
We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties.
## Data Security
All data is transmitted securely using HTTPS encryption.
## Children's Privacy
Our App does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
## Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
